About FITNESS FOUR

The Future of Fitness, Powered by Experience

FITNESS FOUR is the visionary new gym brand under the umbrella of FUEL AND FLEX PRIVATE LIMITED. With over two decades of expertise in the industry, our parent company, Deshna Group, has built a diverse and successful portfolio encompassing renowned ventures in salon and beauty services and notably, fitness and gym services. This rich and proven background in lifestyle and wellness forms the solid bedrock of FITNESS FOUR. Our core mission at FITNESS FOUR is to deliver world-class fitness facilities, cutting-edge equipment, and expert guidance that is truly accessible to all, irrespective of location. We believe that premium fitness experiences shouldn't be confined to metropolitan hubs. We meticulously design inspiring spaces that empower every member to discover and achieve their personal path to a healthier, stronger self.

Unrivaled Support

Your Success is Our Mission

At FITNESS FOUR, we offer an unparalleled end-to-end support system designed to guide you through every stage of your entrepreneurial journey. Our dedicated teams provide expertise across all key areas, ensuring a seamless and profitable venture for our partners.

1. Pre-Opening & Infrastructure Development

  • Strategic location and lease negotiation assistance.
  • Architectural & interior design blueprints aligned with our brand.
  • Guidance in sourcing and installing world-class fitness equipment.
  • Comprehensive infrastructure setup: fit-outs, electrical, AC, lighting, plumbing, and more.

2. Human Resources & Staffing Solutions

  • Support in determining ideal team structure.
  • Help in recruiting qualified trainers, sales staff, club managers, and nutritionists.
  • Comprehensive staff training programs covering sales, customer service, and operational protocols.

3. Sales & Membership Management

  • Implementation and training on advanced sales & subscription management software.
  • Ready-to-use branded membership forms and streamlined onboarding process.
  • Dedicated pre- and post-sales support to boost member conversions and retention.

4. Marketing & Financial Facilitation

  • Initial 3-month marketing support including Facebook, Instagram & Google ads.
  • Provision of branded collateral (stationery, forms, assets).
  • Support with MSME registration, bank loans, and EMI setups for customers.
  • Assistance in availing government subsidies where eligible.
