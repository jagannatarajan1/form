About Us

Authentic Taste, Unmatched Aroma — Briyani Boss in Madipakkam

Located in the heart of Madipakkam, Chennai, Briyani Boss is your destination for premium, flavorful biryani made with traditional spices and handpicked ingredients. Whether it's a casual lunch or a family feast, our biryani is crafted to bring people together through taste and tradition. Every pot is cooked with passion, ensuring consistent quality and irresistible aroma in every bite.

We also specialize in Corporate Catering and Party Orders — perfect for offices, events, and celebrations of any scale!

Chef’s Special

Hyderabadi Chicken Biryani

Savor our signature Hyderabadi Chicken Biryani — long-grain basmati rice layered with tender marinated chicken, slow-cooked to perfection with aromatic spices, saffron, and fried onions. A true royal treat from the kitchens of Briyani Boss.

Weekend Favorite

Madras Mutton Biryani

Dive into the rich taste of Madras Mutton Biryani — spicy, bold, and irresistibly aromatic. This Chennai-style biryani is a local favorite, featuring succulent mutton chunks cooked with flavorful masalas and traditional techniques.

Street Style

Spicy Grilled Chicken

Smoky, spicy, and juicy — our Grilled Chicken is marinated in a special spice blend and char-grilled to perfection. Served with lemon and mint chutney, it’s a must-try for meat lovers in Madipakkam!

Non-Vegetarian Delight

Chicken Dum Biryani

A rich and flavorful option for vegetarians — soft paneer cubes layered with fragrant biryani rice and cooked on dum with herbs, saffron, and ghee. A treat that doesn’t compromise on taste or aroma.
